about

nevere is the production name of Dylan Augustave — I'm an independent producer from Orlando making beats for artists.

Shaped by trap drums, heavy low end, drill, boom bap, moody R&B loops, and the music I grew up around, this catalog is my first public push to put finished beats in front of artists looking for something they can write to.

The goal is simple: give artists a starting point, a mood, and enough space to build something real. Right now the priority is getting these beats in the right hands. If a beat fits your project, reach out.
